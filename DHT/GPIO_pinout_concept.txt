The correct formula for the port number is (32p + 8q + r) , wheï»¿e p, q, r are from the pin name  GPIOp_qr.  q is ABCD corresponding to 0123.
For example, pin# 8, GPIO4_C1, port number = (32x4 + 8x2 + 1) = 145.
